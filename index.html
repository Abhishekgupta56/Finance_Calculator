Financial Insights

Understanding SIP

Systematic Investment Plans allow you to invest small amounts regularly, benefiting from rupee cost averaging and compounding over time.

Capital Gains Tax

Short-term capital gains (held less than specified period) are typically taxed higher than long-term gains. Rates vary by asset class.

EMI Breakdown

Your EMI is comprised of principal repayment and interest. Early in the loan term, most of the payment goes toward interest.

FD Interest Rates

Compounding frequency affects your FD returns. Quarterly compounding typically yields higher returns than annual compounding.
